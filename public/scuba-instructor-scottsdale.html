Scuba Diving Courses

Open Water Diver

Start your diving journey with our comprehensive Open Water certification course. Learn essential skills in our heated pool before progressing to open water dives.

  • 4 confined water dives
  • 4 open water dives
  • Digital learning materials
  • All equipment included

Advanced Open Water

Expand your diving skills with 5 different adventure dives including deep diving, navigation, and specialty areas of interest.

  • 5 adventure dives
  • Deep & navigation required
  • Choose 3 elective dives
  • Immediate certification

Rescue Diver

Learn to prevent and manage problems underwater. This challenging course builds confidence and makes you a better buddy.

  • Self-rescue techniques
  • Recognizing stress in others
  • Emergency management
  • Rescue scenarios

Specialty Courses

Enhance your diving with specialty courses including Nitrox, Deep Diver, Wreck Diver, and Underwater Photography.

  • Nitrox (EANx)
  • Deep Diver
  • Wreck Diver
  • Underwater Photography

Refresher Course

Been a while since your last dive? Our refresher course helps you regain confidence and update your skills safely.

  • Skills review
  • Equipment familiarization
  • Pool session
  • Open water dive

Discover Scuba

Try scuba diving in a pool or shallow water with this introductory experience. Perfect for beginners wanting to test the waters.

  • No experience required
  • Pool or shallow water
  • Basic safety introduction
  • Certified instructor supervision
